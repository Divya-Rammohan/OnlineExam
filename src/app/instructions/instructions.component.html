Instructions for taking up the online examination



  1. Choose your preferred language in which you have to take up the exam.
  2. The exam consists of 3 level with 10 mcq's in each level.
  3. The difficulty of the questions increases with the increase in level.
  4. There is a minimum cut off for each level. Only if you score marks above the cut off marks you will be able to attend the next level.
  5. A report card will be generated after the completion of each level.
  6. In case you have not cleared a particular level, you can logout after viewing your reports.

All the best !
